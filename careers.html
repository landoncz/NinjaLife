Youth Coordinator

$10-12 hour
Part-time

Company Description:

Ninja-Life: We are the premier Ninja Warrior and obstacle course facility for kids and adults to play, train, and compete. We are looking for physically fit, high energy, enthusiastic people to join our team and help grow Ninja-Life into a nationally recognized Ninja Warrior/Obstacle course gym.

At Ninja-Life, we believe that building strength in our youth both physically and mentally are important. Even though our youth programs are a big part of our focus, it doesn’t stop there. This also applies to our adults & parents. Within the facility you will find something for everyone from basic beginner to the advanced elite. And the great part about obstacle course training is “it’s fun” and it’s fun the whole family can participate in together.

Position Description:

Youth coordinators are offered the opportunity to join and grow with a new obstacle course facility, the nations fastest growing sport. A Youth Coordinator must enjoy helping and working with kids. Responsibilities may include being in charge of a specific group of as many as 15 kids on a daily basis or entertaining a birthday party. During open gym times you may be required to monitor the floor and assist with proper execution of an obstacle. There may also be times you’re needed to help cover sales or door. Certain individuals filling this position may be required to drive a company van.

Experience & Requirements needed for this position:

  • A passion for health, fitness, and youth development
  • Positive, enthusiastic, energetic personality
  • Ability to work a flexible schedule
  • Build relationships and show hospitality
  • Able to communicate clearly with clients of all ages
  • Basic computer skills
