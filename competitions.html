If wanting to qualify for UNAA Regionals, participating athletes must join the UNAA (Ultimate Ninja Athlete Association) before you compete. Membership Fee is $25 for the season (Aug 2019 - July 2020). Registration available online at www.ultimateninja.net

  • Course: 7-15 obstacles and you are allowed 3 fails before time stops (athlete may still complete the course)
  • Points: Obstacles are 1 point unless noted. 2 point obstacles are more difficult and have a halfway point
