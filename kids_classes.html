Kids Ninja Warrior Training Classes

Another program we will be offering for our youth at Ninja Life will be structured course classes. These classes will be set up for various skill levels. As your skill level improves, you will advance in class grouping.

It is our hope and goal to create a competitive group in each age bracket to represent Ninja Life at ninja obstacle course competitions. This will be the elite, so if that might be your goal, let me know! I'll set your course and help you start preparing now!

Kids Small Group and 1-on-1 Training

If course classes are something that you're interested in but want more direct focus and attention, then we have that too. We will offer small group (3-5) or even 1-on-1 classes.

At pre scheduled times and dates, we will have celebrity ninjas making appearances. During these appearances we will be offering various structured classes as well as meet and greets.
